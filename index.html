Mexican Salad Bowl

In abowl, add rice at the bottom. Place beans, corn, tomato, and lettuce in sections. Add sliced avocado and chicken. Squeeze lime juice and sprinkle salt. Mix before eating.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Boil and drain the spaghetti. In a bowl, mix eggs, cheese and pepper. Cook garlic in a pan. Add hot spaghetti to pan, remove from flame. Mix egg mixture quickly and serve.

Continental Breakfast

Toast the bread and apply butter/jam. Slice the fruits nicely. Boil an egg and peel it. Arrange all on a plate with coffee or juice. Eat fresh and light.

Dutch Baby Pancake

Mix eggs, milk, flour, and sugar. Heat pan in oven, melt butter. Pour batter into hot pan. Bake at 200 decree celcius for 15-20 mins. Top with berries and sugar.
